I am a Teaching Fellow in Games Design & Art at the Winchester School of Art, University of Southampton, UK. I have a background in Web Science, Natural Language Processing, English Literature & Linguistics, and Fine Art Photography.
I'm currently interested in how systems of divination are and could be used in games, and also interested the intersection between technology and magic.

I'm also a PhD researcher in Web Science at the University of Southampton where my research focuses on computer generated novels and their design, and potential readers/users.
My PhD is supervised by Interactive Narrative researcher Prof. David Millard, and Digital Media researcher Dr. Megen Bruin-Molé.
I've been a research assistant on several projects including the Winchester School of Art Creative AI Lab with Games, Toy, & AI researcher Dr. Seth Giddings, and the Nodenoggin SleepR project with Human Centered Design researcher Dr. Adam Procter.
